Switching to Google+

Murat Yildizoglu

Published: 2011/08/15

Posterous does not accept autoposts by mail from/to Google Plus, so some of my articles can now only be seen on my G+ page. I am switching to G+

Moreover, given the complexity of managing Facebook, Twitter, a blog (Posterous was a potential solution for merging these streams) and now G+, I will progressively group everything in the latter stream. You can read my Google+ stream from this address: My GooglePlus.
